# DRL-LEO-Beam-Positioning
The Python code for DRL-Based Beam Positioning for LEO Satellite Constellations with Weighted Least Squares
  * PPO [1]
  * DDQN [2]
  * DQN [3]
  * ALG-B (Beam-Based Positioning) [4]
  * LSTM-WLS [5]
  * Proposed DQN-WLS

[1] J. Schulman, F. Wolski, P. Dhariwal, A. Radford, and O. Klimov, “Proximal policy optimization algorithms,” arXiv preprint arXiv:1707.06347, 2017.

[2] H. van Hasselt, A. Guez, and D. Silver, “Deep reinforcement learning with double Q-learning,” in Proc. AAAI Conf. Artif. Intell., Feb. 2016, pp. 2094–2100.

[3] V. M. et al., “Human-level control through deep reinforcement learning,” Nature, vol. 518, no. 7540, pp. 529–533, 2015.

[4] M. Elsanhoury, J. Koljonen, M. Elmusrati, and H. Kuusniemi, "A Novel Beam-Based Positioning Paradigm Via Opportunistic Signal of Future Massive MIMO LEO Satellite Constellations," 2024 International Conference on Localization and GNSS (ICL-GNSS), Antwerp, Belgium, 2024, pp. 1-5.

[5] I. Sbeity, C. Villien, C. Combettes, B. Denis, E. V. Belmeg, and M. Chafii, "A Novel Satellite Selection Algorithm Using LSTM Neural Networks For Single-epoch Localization," 2023 IEEE/ION Position, Location and Navigation Symposium (PLANS), Monterey, CA, USA, 2023, pp. 105-112.
